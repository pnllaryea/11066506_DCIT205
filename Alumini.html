Alumni

Welcome, Alumni!

As a valued member of our Alumni community, you are an integral part of the Computer Science Department's legacy. Stay connected with your fellow graduates, and learn about exciting opportunities to engage with the current students and faculty.

Stay Connected

Join our Alumni network to stay informed about upcoming events, achievements, and opportunities. Follow us on social media and participate in alumni gatherings to reconnect with old friends and make new connections.

Share Your Success

We are proud of the accomplishments of our Alumni. If you have exciting news, achievements, or career updates, share them with us! Your success stories inspire current students and contribute to the vibrant history of our department.

Get Involved

Consider getting involved with the department's initiatives. Share your expertise, mentor current students, or contribute to the growth and development of the next generation of Computer Scientists. Your insights are invaluable.
